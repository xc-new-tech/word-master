The Story of 'negotiate'

history_edu

Latin: negōtiārī

From *negotium* ("business"), which itself comes from *nec* ("not") + *otium* ("leisure"). The original meaning was "to carry on business."

draw

16th Century English

The word was adopted into English. Its meaning evolved from just doing business to discussing terms to reach an agreement or bargain.
